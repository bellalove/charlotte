About

about

So you've seen a whole, awesome smorgasbord of everything local from amazing artists, exceptional events and a myriad of media. So the question remains, what is going on here? What is Bella Love? Why do my earphones always get tangled up? We can't really explain the latter but we got those first couple of questions covered.

Bella Love is a one-of-a-kind organization that facilitates community development through social interaction. Using all-local initiatives, community service projects, large and small-scale events and unique social gatherings, Bella Love is connecting all things local like never before.

love who you are…

We all have a passion within us, whether it’s creativity, entrepreneurship or community service. Some passions are overflowing and bursting with inspiration, while others are tucked away, yet to be discovered. Whatever your passion is, Bella Love believes in expressing it. So be proud. Be inspiring. And whatever you do, be YOU.

love what you do…

We all have something unique and original to bring to the table and now is the time to take action! There is an abundance of local talent within our community. From artists and musicians to small-business owners and restaurateurs, Bella Love highlights that talent in a unique, collaborative setting. By bringing the community together to support our area’s exceptional and innovative individuals and businesses, we are fueling inspiration and securing a creative future for generations to come.

love your community…

So how does Bella Love accomplish all this? Starting at the core, Bella Love offers a wide variety of social gatherings that give everyone a chance to get involved. These unique events offer a fresh perspective on art crawls, music collaborations, community service, and evenings of creativity and performance. Bella Love takes local support to a whole new level by bringing Charlotte a physical social network that takes you beyond the virtual realm to make real connections in the community.

Bella Love believes that if we all take ownership and responsibility for our community, there is no limit to what we can accomplish.

“Uniting the community, inspiring local support, and allowing creativity to prosper… That’s Bella Love.”
